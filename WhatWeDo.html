WHAT WE DO ?

Monetary Care

  • Nothing comes without a cost and at SMICT, we understand financial instability is the key reason for most of the children in a country like India to drop out their studies.
  • We have made a huge and stable leap to come forward and provide financial aid to the under privileged students to back their education and ensure MONEY is never a hurdle for anybody to reach their educational goals.

Stationary & Other Basic Needs
  • Not just about direct financial support, at SMICT we realise there are deprived children, presently in need of quality stationary and other basics.
  • To support and motivate these children, we have provided quality stationary items, school bags, exam pads to near by corporation schools.
  • SMICTians are open for any sort of educational support - "Be it BIG or SMALL"

Support For The Special Children
  • SMICT has gone to the next level of social welfare by helping Mentally Challenged children by sponsoring special children homes and procured them furnitures & toys...
  • We live by our Motto - "Called to Serve, not to be Served"
