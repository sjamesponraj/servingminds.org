WHY WE DO ?

"Called to serve, not to be served" is a verse said by Lord Jesus Christ referenced from the Holy Bible. It signifies the mission of Christ coming to this world. Inspired by this verse, we at SMICT craved to consistently provide quality guidance and support to the deprived in our society by some resources. SMICTians have sorted out services related to education.

Service comes obviously, once we start to appreciate everything that has been done for us. As our love for the Christ grows deeper our desire to serve him rises too. It is a natural maturing of our faith that we seek to imitate our Lord in all ways. As he loved and served, so we do love and serve; we do things that put others first even at discomfort and inconvenience to ourselves.
